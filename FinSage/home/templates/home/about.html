About us!

We are a team of motivated individuals who aim to bring positive change through healthy teamwork and collaboration. Harnessing the power of technology and knowledge, we have built a web application to solve the challenge of lack of financial literacy for women and youth in urban and rural India.

Targeting not just the tech-savvy population but also minorities, FinSage allows users to increase their financial literacy through modules and further collaborations with individuals with financial acumen and a drive to change the world through technology.
