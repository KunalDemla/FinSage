Tax Planning

What is tax planning?

Tax planning is the process of managing your finances in a way that minimizes your tax liability. It involves understanding the tax laws, identifying tax deductions and credits, and making financial decisions that will reduce your tax bill.

Tax planning for salaried individuals

Tax planning for salaried individuals is the process of making investment decisions that will minimize your tax liability. There are a number of tax-deductible investments available to salaried individuals, such as the Employees' Provident Fund (EPF), the Public Provident Fund (PPF), and the National Pension System (NPS). By making wise investment choices, salaried individuals can save money on their taxes and build a strong financial foundation for the future.
