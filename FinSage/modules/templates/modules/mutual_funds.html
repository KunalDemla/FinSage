Mutual Funds

Introduction to Mutual Funds

A mutual fund is a type of investment that pools money from many investors and invests it in a variety of assets, such as stocks, bonds, and money market instruments. Mutual funds are managed by professional fund managers who are responsible for selecting the investments that will be included in the fund.

How to invest in mutual funds

Some of the basic steps include understanding your risk appetite, doing your research, choosing your broker and making an investment. One must be careful while investing in mutual funds and must read all scheme related documents carefully.
