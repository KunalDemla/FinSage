Budgeting

What is a budget?

A budget is a plan for how you will spend your money. It helps you track your income and expenses, ensuring you are not spending more than what you earn. Budgeting can help you save money, reach your financial goals, and avoid debt.

Importance of budgeting

Budgeting is essential as it helps you track your income, expenses, save your money and reach your financial goals.

50-30-20 rule

The 50-30-20 rule is a budgeting guideline that suggests dividing your after-tax income into three categories: 50% for needs, 30% for wants and 20% for savings.
