Retirement Planning

How to plan for retirement?

Retirement planning is the process of ensuring that you have enough money to live comfortably after you retire. It is important to start planning for retirement early, as the earlier you start, the more time your money has to grow.

Types of retirement plans

Some of the retirement plans in India include govt sponsored schemes, employment based pension plans, EPF's,PPF's FD's etc. Saving for the future is important so that one can lead a relaxing life after their employment.
